SERENADE EVERLASTING MIXTAPE

soundcloud spotify vizualizer

1. timelapse intro

timelapse intro produced by vainhardt, i knew i had to start this tape off with something familiar and it came out a lot better than i expected so that was fun.

2. serenade everlasting

serenade everlasting produced by me, fun fact despite this being the first full song it was the last to be recorded. the switch up was gonna fully be dubstep but i said fukit im tryna JUMP

3. it could concern

it could concern produced by kilolit and myself, the original beat was on kilo's channel and i said holup i could do sum with this so made the rock instrumental. which makes the initial beat sound more like a prelude and interlude. rather than it being the actual beat like the next song.

4. momentary

momentary produced by narcix and myself, this was my first release of me blending my own elements to someone else's beat.- side note: "live like you" was supposed to be the first with a dubstep intro from vain but i didn't have money to lease more than one beat on that EP. maybe the OG intro version will come out someday.

5. songbird

songbird produced by me, the whole beat is an interpolation(with my own twists ofc) of a song by iamerror. who is a nintendocore OG from before my time. most songs in that genre are way too ahead of their time. it sounds like if emo kids and video game nerds made a school club.

6. parasite daylight

parasite daylight produced by me, the song name is just a flip of the 2008 metalcore band deadbeat nightlife. though, i couldnt really find the exact words. still sounded cool. the sample is also from one of their earliest songs so go-figure.

7. beautiful freestyle

beautiful freestyle produced by narcix and myself, this came about the same way 'it could concern' did. i heard this narcix beat slowed n sat on it for a long time til i eventually knew what to do with it 2 years later. i wanted the name and beginning beat to be intentionally misleading. even though it is an actual freestyle and narcix's original beat is beautiful, the freestyle it isn't the rapping kind that anyone would expect. i've never seen anyone use the word freestyle for other genres, but maybe it's been done before.

8. seed

seed produced by yandere, the most personal song on here lyrically.

if you've read this far thank you, i appreciate anyone who gives this tape a listen. it may be my best yet
